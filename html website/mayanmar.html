Bagan, Myanmar Travel Guide

Bagan, Myanmar, is an ancient city known for its vast plain dotted with thousands of centuries-old temples and pagodas. Here's a travel guide to help you make the most of your trip to this remarkable destination:

  1. Best Time to Visit:

    The ideal time to visit Bagan is from November to February, during the cool, dry season, which offers perfect weather for sightseeing. Avoid March to May due to extreme heat, while the Monsoon season (June to October) brings fewer tourists and greener landscapes.

  2. Must-See Temples:

    Donâ€™t miss Ananda Temple, Shwezigon Pagoda, Dhammayangyi, Sulamani, and Htilominlo temples, each with unique history and architecture.

  3. Top Experiences:

    Hot air balloon rides, scenic sunrise/sunset spots, e-bike exploration, and Ayeyarwady River cruises offer unforgettable experiences.

  4. Etiquette:

    Dress modestly, cover shoulders and knees, and respect temple rules, such as removing shoes and being mindful of photography restrictions.

  5. Where to Stay:

    Old Bagan for luxury, New Bagan for mid-range, and Nyaung U for budget-friendly options.
